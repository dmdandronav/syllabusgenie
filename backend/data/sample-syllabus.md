# AP Chemistry — Unit 3: Thermodynamics

## Key Concepts
- Enthalpy (H): heat content of a system at constant pressure
- Entropy (S): measure of disorder/randomness
- Gibbs Free Energy (G = H - TS): predicts spontaneity

## Important Equations
- ΔH = H_products - H_reactants
- ΔG = ΔH - TΔS (T in Kelvin)
- Spontaneous when ΔG < 0

## Exam Topics (Week 8)
- Hess's Law calculations
- Calorimetry problems (q = mcΔT)
- Predicting spontaneity from ΔH and ΔS signs

## Practice Problem Types
1. Calculate ΔH using bond enthalpies
2. Determine if a reaction is spontaneous at different temperatures
3. Calorimetry: find heat released from temperature change
